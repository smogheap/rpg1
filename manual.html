Objective

To win RPG1, you must find your way to the throne and obtain the crown. You will likely need help from many other characters.

You choose who you are at the beginning, but you can play many roles throughout the game.

RPG1 is a different adventure every time you play. Each new game is a new world, and the world will seem different depending on who you are.

How to Play

RPG1 can be played using:
  • keyboard
  • gamepad or joystick
  • touch screen
  • mouse

Menu / Dialog Controls

When an arrow cursor appears on screen, you may move it to your selection using:
  • keyboard: wasd, arrows, numpad
  • gamepad: directional pad/stick
  • touch screen: n/a
  • mouse: pointer movement
To make your selection:
  • keyboard: ctrl, space, enter
  • gamepad: any push button
  • touch screen: tap your choice
  • mouse: click

Field Controls

When you are exploring the game world, you may move around using:
  • keyboard: wasd, arrows, numpad
  • gamepad: directional pad/stick
  • touch screen: tap destination
  • mouse: click destination
When you are in contact with other characters, you may interact with them using:
  • keyboard: ctrl, space, enter
  • gamepad: any push button
  • touch screen: tap the character
  • mouse: click the character
When you are not in contact with any other characters, you can use the same key/button or click/tap yourself to pull up your personal menu.

Your Personal Menu

From your personal menu, you can take several actions. Some of these depend on what items or abilities you have.

Save and Quit

You may save and quit the game at any time. From the main menu, you can select "CONTINUE" to pick up where you left off.

View Map

If you have a map, you may look at it at any time. Darkened areas indicate places you have traveled to, green spots represent special-purpose areas, and the blinking dot is your current position.

View Trophies

If you have earned any trophies, you may see them at any time.
